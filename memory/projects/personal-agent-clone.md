## Dual Purpose

1. **Personal agent clone** — an agent that can work, think, and create like Ned. Could answer questions about his work, generate design briefs in his voice, evaluate creative work against his standards.

2. **New portfolio website** — use the archived content to build a new site that explains Ned's skills and shows all the work in a fresh way. Potentially targeting creative roles at AI companies (Anthropic, etc.) where the intersection of world-class design craft and deep technical/protocol fluency is rare and valuable.

## Why Ned Is Interesting To AI Companies

Most creative directors at his level don't understand protocols, on-chain identity, or agent economics. Most protocol engineers can't design a Nike campaign. Ned does both. That's a rare profile — someone who can design the brand, the product, the identity system, AND understand the underlying technology at a protocol level. At a company like Anthropic, this would mean someone who can make AI products not just functional but culturally resonant, someone who understands both the technology and the humans it serves.
