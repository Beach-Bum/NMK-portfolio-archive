## What Makes Ned Distinctive

**Breadth:** Graphic design, interiors/architecture, digital product, brand identity, spatial design, print, motion, data visualization, zines, experimental work. Not a specialist — a polymath.

**Scale:** Nike global campaigns, Jordan Brand, Olympic teams, luxury (Givenchy, Rimowa, LVMH), tech (Magic Leap, Status/Logos), retail (IKEA), automotive (Hyundai, SEAT). Consistently operates at the highest level across industries.

**Collaborators:** Virgil Abloh, Kim Jones, Ricardo Tisci, Travis Scott, FKA twigs, Carli Lloyd, Paula Radcliffe, Shoe Surgeon. Works with culture-defining creatives.

**Design Philosophy:** Systems thinking. Builds brand identities, not just logos. Designs the rules, not just the artifacts. The Logos rebrand (from cypherpunk brutalism to optimistic lambda identity) demonstrates ability to make complex, technical, ideological projects accessible through design.

**Technical Fluency:** Unlike most creative directors at his level, Ned has deep crypto/decentralization fluency. He's not just branding Web3 — he understands protocols, economic models, agent economies, and designs from that understanding.

**Current Interest:** Decentralized agent economies. Building a protocol for permissionless agent-to-agent commerce (A2A + x402 + DIDs). Also exploring personal agent clones — capturing his creative process, thinking, and aesthetic to create an autonomous agent version of himself.
