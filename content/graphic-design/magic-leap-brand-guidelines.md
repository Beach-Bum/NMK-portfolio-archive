# Magic Leap Brand Guidelines

**Section:** Graphic Design / Creative Direction
**Original URL:** https://ned-karlovich-slyk.squarespace.com/magic-leap-brand-guideliens

## Description

Comprehensive brand guidelines system for Magic Leap — a 175-page visual identity framework covering logo usage, typography, color systems, photography direction, iconography, layout principles, and tone of voice. This foundational document governed all brand expression across the spatial computing company.

Role: Brand Creative Director, Magic Leap
