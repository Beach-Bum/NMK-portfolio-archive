This brand is inspired by a simple belief: A party for every body each day. YAAYAS is driven by a passion for art, fun and luxury combined with everyday cleansing and fragrance products. A global, beauty care brand for the luxury market.The YAAYAS juxtaposes luxury and fun within materials,language and visuals. We envision how a birthday party meets Coco Chanel under the shower.
