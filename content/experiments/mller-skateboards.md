## Description

Mller Skateboards

We create limited edition hand made skateboards. Each are made from rare and exotic materials from an old lumber yard with no name that is equally as weird as our mill. We pick out as much crazy wood as we strap to our bikes and ride back to the mill. Each deck is made uniquely based on however Simon is feeling that day, so each one is completely unique of course.

These little guys take some time to make because it is all done by hand. Everything is cut, shaped, and finished using rusty tools and four pairs of average eyes. There is no big machine named "Betsy" that bashes out skateboards - just some time and hard work to chase down our youth.

We are totally about creating something beautiful and fun. For a few minutes you a can fly freely with a warm sun and a smile on your face.
